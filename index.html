Our Mission

Confidence on the mats.
Carried everywhere else.

Our mission is to equip you — or your child — with the tools and support to thrive at every stage of your jiu-jitsu journey.

Our welcoming environment is built for everyone, whether you're a seasoned grappler or stepping onto the mat for the very first time. Our members represent a diverse community, starting at age 4 with no upper limit.

We understand that starting jiu-jitsu can feel intimidating. That's why we built the 10PLI 8-Week Beginner Course — so you start alongside other newcomers in a comfortable, supportive introduction to the art.

The Right Way In

Start jiu-jitsu alongside
other beginners — not on your first day rolling with a black belt.

The 10PLI 8-Week Beginner Course is our onboarding program. Learn the fundamentals safely, build real confidence, and finish ready to jump into regular class.

  • FREE 30-minute private intro lesson — learn the ABCs of jiu-jitsu before you ever step into a group class.
  • 16 classes over 8 weeks — 2 classes per week with other brand-new students.
  • Signature 10PLI rash guard — your uniform to wear to class, included in the program.
  • Full facility access — infrared sauna and cold plunge included, available 7 days a week.
