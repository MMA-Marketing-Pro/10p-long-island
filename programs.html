Programs
Built For Everyone.

AM & PM classes, 7 days a week. From absolute beginners to seasoned competitors, from ages 4 to 65+, there's a class here for you.

01
FLAGSHIP · 8 WEEKS

8-Week Beginner Course

Our flagship onboarding program. Free 30-minute private intro lesson. 16 group classes over 8 weeks. Your 10PLI rash guard. Full facility access including sauna and cold plunge. Start jiu-jitsu alongside other beginners — not on your first day rolling with a black belt.

02
AM · PM · 7 DAYS

Adult No-Gi Jiu-Jitsu

Whether you're a beginner or looking for a competitive edge, our no-gi jiu-jitsu classes will meet your needs. Training is available both AM & PM with classes 7 days a week to meet varying schedules. Fundamentals for new students, Mixed Level for everyone ready to roll.

03
AGES 5 TO 12

Kids No-Gi Jiu-Jitsu

Building discipline, getting exercise, and learning jiu-jitsu in a fun and engaging way — all in one. Classes are split by age: Kids 5–7 and Kids 8–12 run Tuesday and Thursday afternoons plus Saturday morning. Coaches who know how to keep kids engaged, challenged, and safe.

04
BEGINNER + ADVANCED

MMA

Blend your jiu-jitsu with striking. Beginner MMA runs Sunday afternoons and Advanced MMA runs Friday nights — both taught by coaches who understand the full game. Add Sunday's Jiu-Jitsu Striking class and you've got a complete striking-plus-grappling week.

05
AGES 35+

Masters 35+ Jiu-Jitsu

Train hard, train smart, and train sustainably. A dedicated Sunday-morning class for the 35+ crowd where technique and longevity come first. Lower-impact drilling, controlled rolls, and partners who understand the masters mindset — plus full access to all other classes.

06
INCLUDED WITH MEMBERSHIP

Personal Training

Get strength and conditioning, physical therapy advice from in-house professionals, and coaching from jiu-jitsu black belts who understand your journey. Whether you want to rehab an injury, build strength for competition, or just get in better shape — we'll build you a plan.

07
INCLUDED · 7 DAYS A WEEK

Recovery — Infrared Sauna & Cold Plunge

Keep your sweat going, detox, and feel great by sitting in our infrared sauna before or after practice. Fight off injuries and heal quicker with our cold plunge. Both are included with every membership — no add-ons, no extras. Use them whenever you like.
