Our Philosophy

Built for everyone who walks through the door.

10th Planet Long Island is a no-gi jiu-jitsu academy. Our members range from ages 4 and up, from complete beginners to seasoned competitors, and we train the 10th Planet no-gi system — one of the most respected and battle-tested grappling systems in the world.

We take pride in building a culture where all people feel welcomed yet challenged in pursuing their jiu-jitsu journey. This isn't just a gym — it's a community that supports each other on and off the mats.

Common Questions

Everything you're wondering
before your first class.

Will I train with other people at my skill level?
Yes. Our beginner program is filled with other people just like you. All of our students have access to our Fundamental Classes, and we do our best to partner you with someone that will help you along the way — not drown you on day one.
What should I wear and bring with me?
A gym shirt and shorts — tighter-fitting for men, or leggings for women. A water bottle and a positive attitude works great too. If you join the 8-week beginner course, you'll get your 10PLI rash guard included.
What happens after I fill out the form?
Someone from our office staff will reach out to help you find a class time that works. You'll get a free private intro lesson where you'll learn the ABCs of jiu-jitsu. Then you'll jump in our Fundamentals class with the rest of the team and learn some more.
Do you have kids classes?
Yes — ages 5–7 and 8–12 have dedicated kids classes running twice a week, plus Saturday morning. Kids learn discipline, get exercise, and pick up jiu-jitsu in a fun and engaging environment.
Is the sauna and cold plunge included?
Yes — infrared sauna and cold plunge access are included with every membership. Use them before or after class, or on your off-days. They help with recovery and injury prevention.
Do you offer MMA or just jiu-jitsu?
Both. Beginner MMA runs Sunday afternoons and Advanced MMA runs Friday nights — both taught by coaches who understand the full game. Striking is a separate Sunday class. Our bread and butter is no-gi jiu-jitsu.
